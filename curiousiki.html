Curiousiki is an obstructive search engine designed to force the user to engage with the information they wish the acquire rather than simply receive it at their leisure.

Taking the database of the largest source of information avaliable to the public, Wikipedia, the engine offers the user the chance to delve further into the topic by 'Digging'. While each dig will be directly related to the page currently open, the user has no choice in the pathway through the information.
The project aims to push users outside of their usual habits and interests when reading up on a subject, and to bring to light new connections that may have not been immediately obvious to them.
