OPENING ACT PROMOTIONS
Contents was a project in altering design narratives. Using the algorhythm behind the website Curiousiki, two conflicting subjects stemming from the same origin were paired alongside each other.

To further explore this concept, the delivery of the information was pushed to it's limits.
First, the subjects were paired into a publication, establishing a linear narrative. Once this was done, the imagery was paired with the text and placed into a 'Cootie Catcher' to give the user a new visual medium to explore while considering the textual elements. Finally, the text was placed alongside a flattened net of these paper toys as a poster, in order to present the entirity of the visual element at once in order to force critical thinking in the viewer while they digested the connection between text and image.
