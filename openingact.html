OPENING ACT PROMOTIONS
Between 2016 and 2017 I worked with Opening Act Promotions to develop a series of gig posters for several events in and around Manchester.
In order to stand out from the sea of posters for similar events, I opted to pay homage to pulpy comic covers, which was a lot of fun. It's always good when a project lets you stretch out into your other interests and influences.
