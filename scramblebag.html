OPENING ACT PROMOTIONS
Working with Manchester Art Gallery, the #Scramblebag was developed as a new tool to help the public connect with art in the gallery space.
Understanding that the gallery can be an intimidating concept to those not familiar with it, the 'Scramble' was designed as a tool for comfort, but also to spark ideas while avoiding the stigma of being seen as a toy.
The #Scramblebag is currently avaliable for use at Manchester Art Gallery every Wednesday and Thursday evening.
